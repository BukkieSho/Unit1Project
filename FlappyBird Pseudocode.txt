Game:
Tap the screen to make bird fly.Stay in the middle of pipes.

Steps:

1. Tap the arrow to start. Arrow is an eventListener with button in HTML doc.

2. Tap the screen again to allow your bird to fly and to start the game. Same effect as the arrow but object (bird) displays motion on tap.

3.Stay in the middle of screen until the first set of pipes appears. Screen moves 200px to the right where class"pipes" are activated.

4. Player measures tap heights to go between the two pipes. Function created for this.

5. Tap fast and go higher. eventListener on tap to reduce height distance between pipes.

6. When player hits bottom of top pipe, bird drops to lower pipe height/ground.

7. Game ends with innerTextHTML
